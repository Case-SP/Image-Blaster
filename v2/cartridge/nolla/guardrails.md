# Content Guardrails

## Sensitivity Tiers

### Tier 1: Safe (Always OK)
- Nature and landscapes
- Food and beverages
- Objects and products
- Abstract concepts
- Architecture and interiors

### Tier 2: Caution (Review Context)
- People in professional settings
- Exercise and fitness imagery
- Medical/health visualization

### Tier 3: Avoid
- Political imagery
- Religious symbols
- Controversial topics
- Violence or conflict
- Explicit content

## Brand Alignment

- Maintain professional, editorial quality
- Avoid overly casual or amateur aesthetics
- Keep compositions clean and uncluttered
- Prefer natural over artificial lighting
